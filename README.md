# MangaForge

**A next-generation digital library engine for curating, reading, and preserving manga collections with offline resilience and cross-device harmony.**

---

## Overview

MangaForge is not just another reader—it is a personal archive system designed for collectors who value ownership, accessibility, and performance. Inspired by the robust architecture of MangaFire, MangaForge reimagines how digital comics are stored, navigated, and experienced. It transforms scattered downloads into a unified, searchable, and beautifully rendered library that works anywhere, even when the internet does not.

At its core, MangaForge functions as a **self-contained curation engine**. It ingests manga from compatible sources, organizes metadata intelligently, and presents each volume with a reading experience optimized for speed and clarity. Whether you are building a permanent offline collection or simply want to read without interruptions, MangaForge treats your library as a living artifact—not a temporary cache.

> *Your library should travel with you, not depend on a server.*  
> *MangaForge makes that belief a reality.*

---

## Why MangaForge?

Most manga readers treat content as ephemeral—fetched on demand, forgotten when closed. MangaForge takes the opposite approach: it treats every chapter as a resource worthy of preservation. By focusing on **offline-first architecture**, **responsive rendering**, and **synchronization without lock-in**, MangaForge gives you control over your collection without compromising on speed or usability.

This is not a tool for quick browsing. This is a **library system** for people who care about what they read.

## Key Features

### 📚 Offline-First Library Engine  
Everything you download stays yours. MangaForge stores chapters locally using an efficient, compressed format that balances readability with storage economy. No internet? No problem. Your library is always accessible.

### ⚡ Performance-Optimized Reader  
Renders pages with sub-second latency, even for high-resolution scans. The reader uses preloading algorithms to anticipate your next move, reducing wait times to zero. Smooth pagination, crisp zoom, and minimal memory footprint.

### 🔄 Seamless Library Synchronization  
Keep your collection in sync across devices without vendor lock-in. MangaForge supports manual export, incremental sync, and metadata-only backups. Your progress, bookmarks, and tags follow you—without requiring a central server.

### 🧠 Intelligent Metadata Management  
Automatically fetches and organizes titles, authors, genres, and chapter numbers. Supports custom tagging, collections, and search filtering by any metadata field. You can even add personal notes to any chapter or volume.

### 🌐 Multilingual Interface  
The entire interface supports over 12 languages, including English, Japanese, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, Korean, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Arabic, and Turkish. Localization extends to metadata and search queries.

### 🎨 Responsive UI with Adaptive Themes  
Works on desktops, tablets, and phones. The UI automatically adjusts to screen size and orientation. Choose from light, dark, sepia, or custom accent themes. Every font, spacing, and color is customizable.

### 📦 Batch Management & Export  
Select entire series or custom ranges for batch operations. Export your library to standard formats (CBZ, PDF, or raw images) for maximum portability. No proprietary lock-in—your collection is always yours to move.

### 🛡️ Integrity Verification  
Every downloaded chapter includes a checksum that MangaForge uses to verify file integrity. Corrupted or incomplete downloads are flagged automatically. Your archive stays healthy.

## Getting Started

To begin using MangaForge, simply acquire the application for your operating system. No registration, no accounts, no data collection. Download the appropriate package and launch it.

Upon first launch, you will be greeted by the **Library Setup Wizard**, which guides you through:
1. Selecting a storage location for your manga archive.
2. Configuring synchronization settings (if desired).
3. Importing existing collections or starting fresh.
4. Choosing your interface language and theme.

From there, you can immediately start adding content. MangaForge will handle metadata fetching, chapter organization, and indexing automatically.

## Architecture & Design Philosophy

MangaForge is built on three pillars:

1. **Ownership** – You control your files. No DRM, no phoning home, no forced updates.
2. **Performance** – Every millisecond counts. The reader is optimized for instant page loads, even with thousands of chapters.
3. **Simplicity** – Complex features should not mean complex interfaces. MangaForge hides power under intuitive gestures.

The codebase is modular, with clear separation between the **storage layer**, **metadata engine**, **reader renderer**, and **synchronization module**. This allows for easy community contributions and custom integrations.
